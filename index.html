  how training works
  
Your English is translated by an LLM (through your OpenRouter key) into a JavaScript
  function reward(prev, action, state). The agent is then trained from scratch with tabular
  Q-learning, in your browser: it plays tens of thousands of times, starting with random moves
  (ε-greedy, decaying) and updating a table Q(state, action) ← Q + α·(r +
  γ·max Q(next) − Q) after every step. The video is the final greedy policy —
  whatever you see, the reward you wrote caused it.
